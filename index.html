OUR COMMITMENTS

POLISH MARK
We support local suppliers for whom the quality and freshness of ingredients are the most important. We use the best Polish products, which we combine with passion for oriental cuisine. By purchasing our products, you support Polish entrepreneurs.
LOCAL SUPPLIERS
We cooperate with proven, local suppliers who comply with the supply chain and hygiene rules. We make deliveries several times a week, so we can be sure that our customers will always receive the fresh products they know. This makes our kebab taste equally good everywhere.
FRESH INGREDIENTS
We prepare everything ourselves from scratch. We only use fresh vegetables provided by local suppliers. The meat is prepared especially for our chain according to an original recipe.
